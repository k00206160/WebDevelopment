Creative Digital Media

What is Creative Digital Media

Creative Digital Media is the combination of Art and design with the complexity of computer technologies and programming.


Introduction

Creative Digital Media is a means of using a persons creative side within the design aspect of media and also having people use their problem solving side to figure out complex resolutions to programming problems. This allows users to have the all around ability to create nice designs for projects such as websites and still have the programming skills to be able to run the backend of the website if anything was to happen to it.
